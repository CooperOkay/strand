# Security

I wrote this in a weekend. It's probably full of holes.

If you find something stupid — a crash, a leak, a way to break it — open an issue or PR. I'll fix it.

If you think the whole approach is wrong and want to argue about it, also open an issue. I'd rather hear why something sucks from someone who tried it than wonder why nobody uses it.

Don't run this on anything important without understanding what it does. It proxies objects, tracks property access, and runs functions. It's not audited. It's not battle-tested. It might eat your data. You've been warned.
